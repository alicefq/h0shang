Travel to ... the Caucasus

Georgia

Georgia is a beautiful country with many high mountains and lots of great nature experiences.

Here are some great things to do in Georgia:

  • Visit the place where 2 rivers meet
  • Visit a cool statue
  • Visit the capital city

Armenia

Armenia is next to Georgia and also has some great nature, including a large lake where you can take boat trips. It also has many cool caves to explore.
